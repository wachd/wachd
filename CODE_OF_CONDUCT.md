# Community Guidelines

## Our Commitment

Wachd is an open project. Everyone who participates — by filing issues, writing code, reviewing PRs, or joining discussions — is expected to treat others with respect.

## Expected Behaviour

- Use welcoming, inclusive language.
- Disagree on ideas, not on people.
- Accept constructive feedback graciously.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruptive behaviour in discussions or reviews.

## Scope

These guidelines apply to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where you represent this project.

## Enforcement

Incidents can be reported to **info@ntcdev.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these guidelines, or ban contributors whose behaviour is harmful to the community.
